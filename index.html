About us

NBJ Trading Company is a supplier company of high quality raw construction materials located in the heart of Central Luzon, Tarlac City. A Christ centered group of people, or what we call family, who looks to glorify God in everything that we do. We are a customer focused company and we offer everything from Silica, Sand, Gravel among others.

Our Vision

NBJ Trading aims to be a premier and leading trade company in the Philippines and to build a great reputation in the industry thus impacting our community by becoming a role model for responsible trading and good governance.

Our Mission

To bring our vision into a fruitful reality, we are devoted to a continuous lifelong learning and put our heart into our business and promotes the well-being of our employees and build long term relationships with our customers thus being part of a more connected community.
